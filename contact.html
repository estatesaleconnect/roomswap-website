Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

No appointment needed during regular business hours. Just stop by! For large estate pickups or after-hours consultations, give us a call to schedule.

What should I bring?

For consignment evaluation, bring photos of larger items or the actual pieces if they're portable. We can assess most items on the spot.

Do you deliver?

Contact us about delivery options for larger furniture purchases. We can discuss arrangements based on your location and item size.

How quickly do items sell?

It varies by item, but quality pieces priced right typically move within a few weeks. We'll keep you updated on interest and pricing adjustments if needed.
