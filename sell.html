Sell or Consign With Us

Have quality furniture, estate contents, or jewelry you're ready to part with? Room Swap gives you two easy options to turn your items into cash — no yard sales, no online listings, no dealing with buyers yourself.

We serve sellers and consignors from Holly Hill, Charleston, Summerville, Goose Creek, Moncks Corner, Orangeburg, St. George, Santee, and across the South Carolina Lowcountry.

Two Ways to Get Paid

We Buy Outright

Need cash now? We purchase select items directly for immediate payment. No waiting, no consignment period.

  • Quality furniture pieces
  • Estate contents — one piece or an entire home
  • Gold, silver, and jewelry
  • Fair cash offers based on market value
  • Same-day payment available

Consign With Us

Want to maximize your return? Let us sell it for you. We handle pricing, display, and sales — you get paid when it sells.

  • 40% consignor payout when items sell
  • We handle pricing, display & sales
  • 4,000 sq ft showroom with steady foot traffic
  • Payouts via check, Venmo, or PayPal
  • No hassle — we do the work

What We're Looking For

Furniture

Quality pieces in good condition — sofas, chairs, tables, dressers, beds, cabinets, and more. Vintage and contemporary styles welcome.

Estate Contents

We can buy one piece or the entire contents of an estate. Contact us to discuss larger purchases and pickup arrangements.

Jewelry

We buy gold, silver, and jewelry. Bring items by for a free evaluation and fair offer based on current market rates.

Home Decor & Vintage

Artwork, lamps, mirrors, collectibles, vintage apparel, vinyl records, and unique items with character and appeal.

What we typically don't accept: Items with significant damage, stains, or wear; outdated electronics; incomplete furniture sets. Mattresses. China hutches. Used appliances.

How to Get Started

1. Contact Us

Call, email, or stop by during business hours. Text photos of items for consignment or selling consideration or free valuation.

2. We Evaluate

We'll assess your items for quality, condition, and market demand. Honest feedback — we'll tell you what we can work with.

3. Get Paid

For outright purchases, get paid on the spot. For consignment, we sell it and you receive your payout via check, Venmo, or PayPal.

Ready to Sell?

The easiest way to get started is to give us a call or stop by with photos of your items. We'll give you an honest assessment and explain your options.
