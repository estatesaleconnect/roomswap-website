Your Options, Clearly Explained

Every estate is different. After our consultation, we'll recommend the best path forward based on the contents, timeline, and your family's needs. Here are the three main approaches we help coordinate:

Direct Buyout

The fastest option. We make a fair cash offer on part or all of an estate's contents and handle removal on our schedule. Ideal when you need to settle quickly or clear a property for sale. No waiting, no commissions — just a straightforward transaction.

Auction

For estates with higher-value items, antiques, or specialty collections, auction can maximize returns. We'll connect you with trusted auction partners in the area who specialize in estate items, and we'll help you understand the process and what to expect.

Estate Sale

A full on-site estate sale can be the best approach for homes with a wide variety of items across all price ranges. We work with experienced estate sale companies who handle pricing, marketing, staffing, and execution — so you don't have to.
