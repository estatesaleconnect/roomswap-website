Welcome to Room Swap

Room Swap Consignments is more than just a furniture store—it's where quality pieces find new homes and new stories begin. Our 4,000 square foot showroom in Holly Hill features carefully selected furniture, home decor, books, vinyl, and vintage treasures that bring character to any space.

Whether you're furnishing your first home, looking for that perfect statement piece, or ready to pass along furniture you no longer need, we're here to help. We buy select items outright and offer consignment services that make it easy to turn your furniture into cash.

What We Offer

Quality Furniture

From vintage finds to contemporary pieces, our rotating inventory features furniture that's built to last. Every piece is inspected for quality before hitting our showroom floor.

Home Decor & More

Discover books, vinyl records, vintage apparel, and unique decorative items that add personality to your space. Our expanding "Book Nook" offers a cozy place to browse.

Buying Services

We buy quality furniture, estate contents, and precious metals. Bring in your items for a fair evaluation, or contact us about larger estate purchases.

Easy Consignment

Turn your quality furniture into cash without the hassle of private selling. We handle everything from pricing to sales, and you get paid when your items sell.
