Welcome to Room Swap

Room Swap Consignments is more than just a furniture store — it's where quality pieces find new homes and new stories begin. Our 4,000 square foot showroom in Holly Hill features carefully selected furniture, home decor, jewelry, books, vinyl, and vintage & modern treasures that bring character to any space.

Whether you're furnishing your first home, looking for that perfect statement piece, or ready to pass along furniture you no longer need, we're here to help. We buy select items outright and offer consignment services that make it easy to turn your preloved pieces into cash. Serving Holly Hill, Charleston, Summerville, Goose Creek, Moncks Corner, Orangeburg, St. George, Santee, and the greater Lowcountry area.

What We Offer

Quality Furniture

From vintage finds to contemporary pieces, our rotating inventory features furniture that's built to last. Every piece is inspected for quality before hitting our showroom floor.

Home Decor & More

Discover artwork, lamps, vases, books, vinyl records, jewelry, apparel, and unique decorative items that add personality to your space. 4,000 sq ft to browse!

Buying Services

We can buy one piece or the entire contents of an estate. Bring in your items for a fair evaluation, or contact us about larger estate purchases.

Easy Consignment & Fast Cash

Turn your quality furniture into cash without the hassle of private selling. We handle everything from pricing to sales, and you get paid when your items sell. We can also make cash offers on many items!
