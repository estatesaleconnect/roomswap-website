Estate Sale Services

Settling an estate is one of the most overwhelming tasks a family can face. Whether you've lost a loved one, are downsizing, or managing a property transition, you shouldn't have to navigate it alone. Room Swap offers free in-home estate consultations to help you understand your options and make informed decisions about the contents of a home.

Free In-Home Consultations

We come to you at no cost and no obligation. We'll walk through the home, assess the contents, and provide an honest evaluation of what you're working with — from furniture and antiques to jewelry, collectibles, and everyday household items.

Expert Valuations

With a decade of experience and millions of dollars in estate items evaluated and sold, we know how to identify value others might miss. Our research-driven approach ensures you get accurate, fair assessments grounded in current market data — not guesswork.

Trusted Industry Connections

We've built strong relationships with reputable auction houses and estate sale companies across the South Carolina Lowcountry. When a particular approach serves you better than what we offer directly, we'll connect you with professionals we personally trust.

Your Options, Clearly Explained

Every estate is different. After our consultation, we'll recommend the best path forward based on the contents, timeline, and your family's needs. Here are the three main approaches we help coordinate:

Direct Buyout

The fastest option. We make a fair cash offer on part or all of an estate's contents and handle removal on our schedule. Ideal when you need to settle quickly or clear a property for sale. No waiting, no commissions — just a straightforward transaction.

Auction

For estates with higher-value items, antiques, or specialty collections, auction can maximize returns. We'll connect you with trusted auction partners in the area who specialize in estate items, and we'll help you understand the process and what to expect.

Estate Sale

A full on-site estate sale can be the best approach for homes with a wide variety of items across all price ranges. We work with experienced estate sale companies who handle pricing, marketing, staffing, and execution — so you don't have to.

How Consignment Works

Consignment at Room Swap is straightforward and hassle-free. You bring in your quality furniture or treasures, we evaluate it, and if it's a good fit for our showroom, we'll display and sell it for you. When it sells, you get paid. No yard sales, no online listings, no dealing with buyers. We handle everything.

1. Bring It In

Stop by during business hours with photos of your items, or bring smaller pieces directly to our showroom. For larger furniture or estate contents, contact us to discuss pickup options.

2. We Evaluate

We'll assess your items for quality, condition, and market demand. We're looking for pieces that are clean, functional, and in good condition. Not everything makes the cut, but we'll give you honest feedback.

3. We Display & Sell

Accepted items go on our showroom floor where they're marketed to our steady stream of customers. We handle pricing, display, and all customer interactions.

4. You Get Paid

When your item sells, you receive your consignment percentage! Standard consignor payout of 40%. Payouts are given as checks, Venmo, or Paypal for flexibility!

What We Accept

We're selective about what goes on our showroom floor because we want every piece to reflect quality and value. Here's what we look for:

Furniture

Quality pieces in good condition including sofas, chairs, tables, dressers, beds, cabinets, and more. Both vintage and contemporary styles welcome.

Home Decor

Lamps, artwork, mirrors, rugs, and decorative items that complement furniture pieces. Items should be clean and in excellent condition.

Books & Media

Collectible books, vintage vinyl records, and interesting media items.

Vintage Items

Vintage apparel, jewelry, collectibles, and unique finds that have character and appeal. Authenticity and condition matter.

What we typically don't accept: Items with significant damage, stains, or wear; outdated electronics; incomplete furniture sets. Mattresses. China hutches. Used appliances.
