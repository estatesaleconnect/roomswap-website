How Consignment Works

Consignment at Room Swap is straightforward and hassle-free. You bring in your quality furniture, we evaluate it, and if it's a good fit for our showroom, we'll display and sell it for you. When it sells, you get paid. No yard sales, no online listings, no dealing with buyers. We handle everything.

The Consignment Process

1. Bring It In

Stop by during business hours with photos of your items, or bring smaller pieces directly to our showroom. For larger furniture or estate contents, contact us to discuss pickup options.

2. We Evaluate

We'll assess your items for quality, condition, and market demand. We're looking for pieces that are clean, functional, and in good condition. Not everything makes the cut, but we'll give you honest feedback.

3. We Display & Sell

Accepted items go on our showroom floor where they're marketed to our steady stream of customers. We handle pricing, display, and all customer interactions.

4. You Get Paid

When your item sells, you receive your consignment percentage. Payment is issued according to our consignment agreement terms. It's that simple.

What We Accept

We're selective about what goes on our showroom floor because we want every piece to reflect quality and value. Here's what we look for:

Furniture

Quality pieces in good condition including sofas, chairs, tables, dressers, beds, cabinets, and more. Both vintage and contemporary styles welcome.

Home Decor

Lamps, artwork, mirrors, rugs, and decorative items that complement furniture pieces. Items should be clean and in excellent condition.

Books & Media

Collectible books, vintage vinyl records, and interesting media items. We're building our Book Nook with curated selections.

Vintage Items

Vintage apparel, collectibles, and unique finds that have character and appeal. Authenticity and condition matter.

What we typically don't accept: Items with significant damage, stains, or wear; outdated electronics; clothing except vintage/collectible pieces; incomplete furniture sets.

We Also Buy Outright

In addition to consignment, we purchase select items directly:

Quality Furniture

We buy furniture pieces that we know will move quickly. Bring items by for evaluation, or contact us about larger collections.

Estate Contents

We handle estate purchases and can arrange pickup for larger quantities. Contact us to discuss your estate sale needs.

Precious Metals

We buy gold, silver, and precious metals using professional testing equipment. Fair prices based on current market rates.

Ready to Get Started?

The best way to see if your items are a good fit is to stop by during our business hours. Bring photos of larger items, or bring smaller pieces directly. We'll give you honest feedback and explain next steps if your items are accepted.
